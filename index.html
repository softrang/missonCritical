MCG COMPANIES

MCG is made up of distinct brands with specialized roles. Our direction creates an unmatched portfolio while ensuring seamless alignment and maximizing synergy for our customers.

Based in Austin, Texas, MCFI is a customer-facing systems integrator that delivers end-to-end and packaged power solutions.

Rooted in Boise, Idaho, JTS specializes in manufacturing generator/equipment enclosures, and modular power systems, ensuring robust protection for critical components.

Located in Belle Chasse, Louisiana, and Bay St. Louis, Mississippi, PEP excels in producing switchgear and other product offerings enhancing system reliability.
